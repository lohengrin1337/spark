Customer
-----
Customers use the system in the following ways:

* Creating an account (web)
* Logging in (web)
* Handling their account information (web)
* Handling their payments (web)
* Finding an available scooter (app)
* Renting and returning a scooter (app)
* Finding parking and charging locations (app)
* Viewing scooters, locations and city limits on a map (app)
* Viewing current and historic rentals/journeys (app/web)
